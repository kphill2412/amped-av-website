## 6. FAQ (`faq.html`)

**Headline:** Frequently asked questions  

### General
- **What areas do you serve?** Dunedin, Clearwater, Palm Harbor, Safety Harbor, Tarpon Springs, Largo, Oldsmar, St. Petersburg, and greater Pinellas County. [Contact us](/contact.html) if you're nearby.
- **Do you offer free estimates?** Yes. We start with a quick walkthrough (in person or virtual) and provide clear pricing before any work begins.
- **Do I work with one person or a team?** One expert, start to finish — recommendation, equipment, install, and making sure everything works.
- **How do I get started?** Text or call for a quote, or use the contact form. We'll schedule a walkthrough and send a clear plan.

### TV mounting
- **Do you hide wires?** Yes. Clean installs with concealed wiring whenever the wall structure allows.
- **Can you mount a TV above a fireplace?** Often yes — we'll assess the wall, viewing height, and heat during the walkthrough.
- **What size TVs do you mount?** Most common sizes from 43" to 85"+. We use appropriate mounts for weight and wall type.

### Audio
- **Do you install Sonos and surround sound?** Yes — soundbars, surround systems, multi-room audio, and outdoor speakers.
- **Can you improve TV sound without a full surround system?** Yes. Soundbars and upgraded speakers are a common request.

### WiFi & networking
- **My WiFi doesn't reach the whole house. Can you fix that?** Yes. Mesh systems, access points, and wired backhaul for reliable coverage.
- **Do you run ethernet cable?** Yes, when needed for stable connections to TVs, offices, and access points.

### Security
- **What kind of cameras do you install?** Ring, Nest, Ubiquiti, and other quality brands — wired or wireless depending on the home.
- **Can I view cameras on my phone?** Yes. We set up and test the app before we leave.

### Pricing & process
- **How much does TV mounting cost in Dunedin?** Depends on TV size, wall type, and wire concealment. Most installs are quoted after a quick walkthrough. *(TODO: add typical range if desired)*
- **How long does installation take?** Most single-room projects are a few hours. Whole-home projects may take a full day or more.
- **Do you sell equipment or install what I already bought?** Both. We can recommend and supply gear, or install customer-supplied equipment when appropriate.
