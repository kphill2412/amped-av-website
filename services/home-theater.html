Common questions

TV or projector?

Both work — we'll recommend based on your room, budget, and how you use the space. Many clients choose a large TV for brightness and simplicity.

Do you design the whole room?

We focus on the AV — screen, speakers, wiring, and integration. We can advise on layout and work alongside your contractor or designer on seating and finishes.

How is this different from TV mounting?

TV mounting is a single display on the wall. Home theater is a coordinated system — video, surround audio, subs, sources, and often a dedicated room setup.
