  • Wall mount selection for your TV size and wall type

Common questions

Do you hide wires?

Yes. We prioritize clean installs with concealed wiring whenever the wall structure allows.

Can you mount above a fireplace?

Often yes — we'll assess wall type, viewing height, and heat exposure during the walkthrough.

What size TVs do you mount?

From 43" to 85" and larger. We match the mount to weight and wall type.
