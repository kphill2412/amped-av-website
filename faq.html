Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about our services, process, and service area in Pinellas County.

General

What areas do you serve?

Dunedin, Clearwater, Palm Harbor, Safety Harbor, Tarpon Springs, Largo, Oldsmar, St. Petersburg, and greater Pinellas County. Contact us if you're nearby and not sure.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. We start with a quick walkthrough (in person or virtual) and provide clear pricing before any work begins.

Do I work with one person or a team?

One expert, start to finish — recommendation, equipment, install, and making sure everything works.

How do I get started?

Text or call (727) 555-0123, or use our contact form. We'll schedule a walkthrough and send a clear plan.

TV mounting

Do you hide wires?

Yes. Clean installs with concealed wiring whenever the wall structure allows.

Can you mount a TV above a fireplace?

Often yes — we'll assess the wall, viewing height, and heat during the walkthrough.

What size TVs do you mount?

Most common sizes from 43" to 85"+. We use appropriate mounts for weight and wall type.

Home audio

Do you install Sonos and surround sound?

Yes — soundbars, surround systems, multi-room audio, and outdoor speakers.

Can you improve TV sound without a full surround system?

Yes. Soundbars and upgraded speakers are a common request.

WiFi & networking

My WiFi doesn't reach the whole house. Can you fix that?

Yes. Mesh systems, access points, and wired backhaul for reliable coverage.

Do you run ethernet cable?

Yes, when needed for stable connections to TVs, offices, and access points.

Security

What kind of cameras do you install?

Ring, Nest, Ubiquiti, and other quality brands — wired or wireless depending on the home.

Can I view cameras on my phone?

Yes. We set up and test the app before we leave.

Pricing & process

How long does installation take?

Most single-room projects are a few hours. Whole-home projects may take a full day or more.

Do you sell equipment or install what I already bought?

Both. We can recommend and supply gear, or install customer-supplied equipment when appropriate.

Ready to upgrade your setup?

Reach out for a quick quote or to talk through your project.
